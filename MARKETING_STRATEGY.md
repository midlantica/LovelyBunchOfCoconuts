# WakeUpNPC Marketing Strategy & Site Analysis

## Site Analysis - Critical Elements

### ✅ What's Working Well

- **Strong Visual Identity**: The Wall pattern is distinctive and memorable
- **Clear Positioning**: Conservative satire of progressive ideology is crystal clear
- **Good Content Mix**: Claims, Quotes, and Memes provide variety
- **Mobile Responsive**: Site works well across devices
- **Fast Performance**: Quick load times, good user experience
- **Engagement Features**: Like system, sharing capabilities

### ⚠️ Potential Improvements

1. **Call-to-Action (CTA)**: No clear "what to do next" for visitors
2. **About/Mission Page**: New visitors might want context about who you are
3. **Contact/Submit Content**: No way for users to submit content or contact you
4. **Newsletter Signup**: Missing opportunity to build an email list
5. **Social Media Links**: No visible social presence/cross-promotion
6. **RSS/API**: No way for other sites to syndicate your content

## Advertising Integration Strategy

### Ad Placement Architecture

```
Current Pattern: [Claim] [Quote] [Meme] [Meme]
With Ads:        [Claim/Ad] [Quote/Ad] [Meme] [Meme]
```

### Frequency Algorithm

```javascript
// Suggested frequency model
const adFrequency = {
  premium: 1 / 4, // 25% - Every 4th slot
  standard: 1 / 8, // 12.5% - Every 8th slot
  budget: 1 / 16, // 6.25% - Every 16th slot
}
```

### Pricing Model

#### CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions)

- **Premium Placement**: $25-40 CPM
- **Standard Placement**: $15-25 CPM
- **Budget Placement**: $8-15 CPM

#### Monthly Flat Rate

- **Premium Package**: $2,500/month (guaranteed 100k impressions)
- **Standard Package**: $1,000/month (guaranteed 50k impressions)
- **Starter Package**: $250/month (guaranteed 15k impressions)

## Risk Mitigation

### Maintain Editorial Independence

- Clear ad labeling
- No advertiser influence on content
- Diverse advertiser base

### User Experience

- Cap ad frequency at 20% max
- No auto-play video/audio
- Mobile-optimized ad sizes

### Platform Risk

- Build email list (own your audience)
- Multiple traffic sources
- Consider app development
