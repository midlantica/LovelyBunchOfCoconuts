## Rowan Atkinson

From the bumbling Mr. Bean to the magnificently scheming Edmund Blackadder, Rowan Atkinson has given the world two of comedy's most beloved characters. His rubber-faced physical comedy and razor-sharp wit have made him a global icon. The Blackadder series, which he co-wrote, is widely considered the finest British sitcom ever made.
