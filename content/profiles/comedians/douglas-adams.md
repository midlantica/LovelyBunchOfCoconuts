## Douglas Adams

Douglas Adams created The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, a comic science fiction masterpiece that began as a BBC radio play and became a global phenomenon. His ability to find cosmic absurdity in the mundane — and mundane absurdity in the cosmic — was unmatched. The answer to life, the universe, and everything is, of course, 42.
