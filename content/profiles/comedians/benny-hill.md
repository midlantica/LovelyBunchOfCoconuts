## Benny Hill

Benny Hill was a global phenomenon whose slapstick comedy and music hall sensibility made him one of the most-watched entertainers on the planet. The Benny Hill Show, with its speeded-up chase sequences and cheeky humour, was broadcast in over 140 countries. A master of physical comedy and comic timing, Hill was a true original.
