## Eric Morecambe

Eric Morecambe, alongside Ernie Wise, formed the greatest double act in British comedy history. The Morecambe and Wise Christmas Show was a national institution, regularly watched by over 20 million people. Eric's genius was his ability to make chaos look effortless — a man perpetually surprised by his own brilliance.
