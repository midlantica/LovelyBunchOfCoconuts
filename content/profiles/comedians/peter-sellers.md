## Peter Sellers

Peter Sellers was a chameleon of comedy, capable of inhabiting any character with uncanny precision. From Inspector Clouseau to Dr. Strangelove, his range was extraordinary. A founding member of The Goon Show alongside Spike Milligan, Sellers helped define post-war British comedy and influenced generations of performers.
