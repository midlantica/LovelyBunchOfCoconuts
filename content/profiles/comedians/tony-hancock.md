## Tony Hancock

Tony Hancock was the quintessential British everyman — pompous, self-deluding, and magnificently tragic. Hancock's Half Hour defined British sitcom in the 1950s and 60s, with Hancock playing a man perpetually convinced he deserved better than life was giving him. His comedy of frustrated aspiration remains deeply relatable.
