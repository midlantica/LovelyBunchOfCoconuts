## Spike Milligan

Spike Milligan was the anarchic genius behind The Goon Show, a radio comedy that shattered every convention and invented surreal British humour as we know it. His influence on Monty Python, The Beatles, and virtually every British comedian who followed is incalculable. Milligan's comedy came from a place of genuine madness, grief, and brilliance.
