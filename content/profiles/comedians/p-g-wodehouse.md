## P.G. Wodehouse

P.G. Wodehouse created Bertie Wooster and Jeeves, the most beloved comic duo in English literature. His prose style — light, musical, and endlessly inventive — has never been surpassed. Wodehouse wrote nearly a hundred books and never wrote a dull sentence, which is either a miracle or proof that some people are simply born funny.
