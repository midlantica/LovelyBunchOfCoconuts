## Victoria Wood

Victoria Wood was a writer, comedian, actress, and singer of extraordinary talent who captured the mundane absurdity of British life with warmth and precision. Her sketches, stand-up, and the sitcom Dinnerladies revealed a comic genius who found the extraordinary in the ordinary. She remains one of Britain's most beloved entertainers.
