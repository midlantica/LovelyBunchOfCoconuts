## Graham Chapman

Graham Chapman was the anarchic heart of Monty Python, best known for his majestic portrayal of King Arthur in Monty Python and the Holy Grail and Brian in Life of Brian. A qualified doctor who rarely practised medicine, Chapman brought an air of genuine authority to the most absurd situations. He was, as his fellow Pythons noted, a very naughty boy.
