## John Cleese

Co-founder of Monty Python and creator of Fawlty Towers, John Cleese is arguably Britain's greatest living comedian. His portrayal of Basil Fawlty — a magnificently incompetent hotel owner — remains one of television's finest comic creations. Cleese's genius lies in his ability to make pomposity and social embarrassment exquisitely funny.
