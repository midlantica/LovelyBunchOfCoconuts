## Stephen Fry

Stephen Fry is the polymath of British comedy — actor, writer, comedian, and national treasure. From his partnership with Hugh Laurie in A Bit of Fry and Laurie to his definitive portrayal of Jeeves, Fry embodies a certain kind of effortlessly erudite British wit. His QI hosting duties made him the nation's favourite know-it-all.
