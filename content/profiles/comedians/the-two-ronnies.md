## The Two Ronnies

Ronnie Barker and Ronnie Corbett formed one of British television's greatest double acts, combining wordplay, sketches, and gentle absurdity in their long-running BBC variety show. Barker's genius for language — particularly his malapropisms and spoonerisms — was matched by Corbett's gift for the rambling, digressive monologue. Together they were unmissable.
