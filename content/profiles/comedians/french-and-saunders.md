## French & Saunders

Dawn French and Jennifer Saunders formed one of British comedy's greatest partnerships, their sketch show running for over a decade and producing some of television's most memorable parodies. Saunders' Absolutely Fabulous and French's The Vicar of Dibley showed that their individual talents were as formidable as their partnership.
