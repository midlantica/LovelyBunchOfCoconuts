![The two totalitarian systems which we in Britain had to fight - Nazism and Communism](/memes/data/the-two-totalitarian-systems-which-we-in.jpg)

The two totalitarian systems which we in Britain had to fight - Nazism and Communism - two socialist tyrannies, let's remember - represented one single model.
The other was represented by our Anglo-American liberal political culture - our parliamentary institutions, our law, our notions of human rights and our free enterprise system based on private property.
