Q: How many non-edge cases of abortions have there been, abortions of choice, in the US?
A: Over 60 Million. Since Roe v. Wade legalized abortion in 1973, the Guttmacher Institute and CDC estimate around 63 million legal abortions in the US through 2022, with nearly all elective (e.g., 93% for socioeconomic reasons per Guttmacher). About 1 million more occurred from 2023-2025.
