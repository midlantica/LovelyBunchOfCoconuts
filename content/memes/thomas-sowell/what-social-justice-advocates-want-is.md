title: "What 'social justice' advocates want is"
---

![What 'social justice' advocates want is](/memes/thomas-sowell/what-social-justice-advocates-want-is.webp)

"What 'social justice' advocates want is not justice, but to be in charge of deciding who gets what. They want to substitute their own decisions for the decisions of millions of other people freely interacting in the marketplace.
"That is why they speak in terms of results rather than rules - of ending up with equal incomes or equal outcomes rather than everyone being equally free to make their own choices. It is not justice; it is power." - Thomas Sowell
