title: 'The fatal attraction of government is that it allows busybodies'
---

![The fatal attraction of government is that it allows busybodies](/memes/thomas-sowell/the-fatal-attraction-of-government-is-th.webp)

"The fatal attraction of government is that it allows busybodies to impose decisions on others without paying any price themselves. No matter how disastrous those decisions turn out to be, the busybodies pay no price. They can always blame it on those who carry out their orders." -Thomas Sowell
