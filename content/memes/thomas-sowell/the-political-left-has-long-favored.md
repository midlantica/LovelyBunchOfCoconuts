title: 'The political Left has long favored'
---

![The political Left has long favored](/memes/thomas-sowell/the-political-left-has-long-favored.jpg)

"The political Left has long favored putting more and more decisions in the hands of people who pay no price for being wrong - not only judges but zoning boards, environmental commissions and, internationally, the United Nations and the World Court. This is a vision of the wise and the virtuous imposing their wisdom and virtue on the lesser people who make up the rest of humanity." - Thomas Sowell
