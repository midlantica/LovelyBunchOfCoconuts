---
title: 'Supreme Court On Free Speech'
---

![Supreme Court On Free Speech](/memes/quotes/supreme-court-on-free-speech.webp)

This United States is founded on a "profound national commitment to the principle that debate on public issues should be uninhibited, robust, and wide-open, and that it may well include vehement, caustic, and sometimes unpleasantly sharp attacks on government and public officials."
Quote from Supreme Court opinion in 'New York Times Co. v. Sullivan', 376 U.S. 254, 1964, by Justice William J. Brennan Jr.
