## "'Equality' is one of the crucial far-fetched ideas of our time. From the soapbox demagogue to the highest court in the land, statistical disparities in outcomes have been treated as virtual proof that somebody was treated unfairly. 

Apparently, there is no way that they could have performed differently, because of the implicit assumption of equality."

Thomas Sowell, *Barbarians Inside the Gates and Other Controversial Essays (1999)*
