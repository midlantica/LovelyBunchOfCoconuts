## "Perhaps the most dangerous ‘favor’ done to blacks has been the making of excuses for all their problems.

All human beings are so imperfect, no matter what color wrapping they come in, that to exempt any group from the standards of performance and behavior expected of others is not a blessing but a curse.

Many white liberals have adopted blacks as mascots, in order to ‘make a statement’ against American society.

But mascots are only symbols — and their well-being is seldom a top priority."

Thomas Sowell
