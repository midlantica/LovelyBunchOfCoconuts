## "Slavery was not confined by race. It was not defined or created by race. It existed for thousands of years.

A history professor once had a student come up to him and ask, ‘When did slavery begin?’ The real question is: when did freedom begin? Slavery existed as long as we have records. From archaeological findings, we know that people were enslaving other people before they could read or write. It has always existed and has existed all over the world.

The number of White people enslaved by pirates in North Africa was greater than the number of Africans brought to the United States.

And yet, that’s not even mentioned."

Thomas Sowell, *C-SPAN Interview*
