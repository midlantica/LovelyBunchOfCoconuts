## The Berlin Wall Falls 1989

On November 9, 1989, the Berlin Wall fell.

It wasn't just a wall. It was the ultimate test:

**Which system works—capitalism or socialism?**

Same people. Same culture. Same history.

One side got freedom and markets.

The other got central planning and secret police.

The results:

**West Berlin**: Prosperity, innovation, freedom. People had cars, food, hope.

**East Berlin**: Poverty, surveillance, despair. People risked death to escape.

The wall wasn't built to keep capitalists out.

It was built to keep socialists _in_.

### When it fell, the world saw what socialists had hidden for 28 years:

- Empty stores
- Crumbling buildings
- Stasi files on millions of citizens
- A system so broken that people would rather die than stay

The fall of the Berlin Wall didn't just end the Cold War.

It ended the argument.

Socialism lost. Freedom won. Case closed.
