## Walter Williams on Minimum Wage

Walter Williams spent decades explaining one brutal truth:

**The minimum wage destroys jobs for the people it claims to help.**

His argument was simple:

If you're worth $10/hour to an employer, but the law says they must pay you $15/hour, what happens?

You don't get hired.

The minimum wage doesn't raise your value. It just makes you unemployable.

Williams showed the data:

- Before minimum wage laws: black teen unemployment was _lower_ than white teen unemployment
- After minimum wage laws: black teen unemployment skyrocketed to 40%+

Why? Because minimum wage laws price low-skilled workers out of the market.

They can't compete on price, so they can't get the entry-level jobs that teach them skills.

Williams called it what it is:

**"The minimum wage law is one of the most anti-black laws on the books."**

Leftists claim they're helping the poor.

Williams proved they're destroying the first rung of the economic ladder—and laughing while they do it.
