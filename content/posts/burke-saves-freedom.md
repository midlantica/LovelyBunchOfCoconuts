## Edmund Burke Saves Freedom

Edmund Burke supported the American Revolution and Catholic rights, but in 1790 he saw terror coming in France.

Revolutionaries were destroying church, monarchy, provinces, guilds—even the calendar—in the name of pure Reason.

Burke predicted the guillotine and Napoleon years before they happened. He was mocked, then proven right.

Freedom, he taught, grows slowly in real soil—manners, religion, property, custom. Rip it out and you get tyranny wearing liberty's mask.

Modern conservatism starts here.
