## Scottish Miracle 1707-1850

In 1700 Scotland was one of Europe's poorest nations—regular famines, almost no industry, life expectancy under 40.

Then everything changed:

- 1707 Union opened England's markets
- Scottish Enlightenment (Hume, Smith) explained wealth
- Merchants unleashed industry

Result: GDP per person grew **fifteen-fold** in a century—the fastest leap ever recorded. Literacy hit 90%. A nation of shepherds gave the world the steam engine and modern economics.

Capitalism didn't need theories; Scotland lived it.
