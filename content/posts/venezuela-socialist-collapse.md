## Venezuela: Socialist Collapse in Real Time

In 1999 Venezuela was the richest country in South America.

Massive oil reserves. Educated population. Modern infrastructure.

Then Hugo Chávez promised "21st Century Socialism."

By 2019, Venezuela was a failed state.

**What happened?**

Socialism. That's what happened.

The timeline:

**2000s**: Chávez nationalizes oil, banks, farms, factories.

**2010s**: Price controls create shortages. Inflation begins.

**2013**: Chávez dies. Maduro continues the policies.

**2016**: Inflation hits 800%. People eat zoo animals.

**2018**: Inflation hits 1,000,000%. Currency becomes worthless.

**2019**: 5 million refugees flee. GDP collapses 75%.

### The excuses from the left:

- "It's the sanctions!" (Collapse started before sanctions)
- "It's the oil prices!" (Other oil exporters didn't collapse)
- "It wasn't real socialism!" (They nationalized everything—what's more real?)

### The reality:

- Price controls created shortages
- Nationalizations destroyed production
- Money printing caused hyperinflation
- Property rights disappeared
- Capital fled

Venezuela had everything: oil, resources, educated people.

Socialism destroyed it all in 20 years.

### The lesson is clear:

**Socialism doesn't just fail in theory. It fails in practice. Every. Single. Time.**

Venezuela is the latest proof.

It won't be the last.
