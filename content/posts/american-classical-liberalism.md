## America's Classical Liberal Era

### The Founders distilled English liberty into plain words:

- Government only stops harm to others
- Power divided so ambition checks ambition
- Property protected above all

For decades the federal government stayed tiny (<2% GDP). Result: from 13 colonies to the world's richest nation in record time.

Tocqueville marveled in 1831: Americans fixed problems themselves—churches, lodges, companies—not by begging the state.

Low taxes, sound money, open borders, minimal regulation = the greatest prosperity surge in history.
