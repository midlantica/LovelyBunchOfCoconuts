## The Rich DO Pay Their Fair Share

The left's favorite lie: "The rich don't pay their fair share!"

The data says otherwise.

### 2021 IRS Data:

- Top 1% earned 22% of income
- Top 1% paid 42% of all federal income taxes

That's nearly double their share of income.

- Top 10% earned 51% of income
- Top 10% paid 74% of all federal income taxes

- Bottom 50% earned 11% of income
- Bottom 50% paid 3% of all federal income taxes

**The rich pay far more than their "fair share."**

### But it gets better:

**Effective tax rates:**

- Top 1%: 26% effective rate
- Top 10%: 20% effective rate
- Bottom 50%: 3% effective rate

The system is already massively progressive.

**"But what about billionaires who pay nothing?"**

### That's a myth. They pay:

- Capital gains taxes (when they sell assets)
- Corporate taxes (on business profits)
- Property taxes (on real estate)
- Payroll taxes (on employees)

### When Jeff Bezos "pays no income tax," it's because:

- His wealth is in Amazon stock (unrealized gains)
- He takes a small salary
- You can't tax wealth that hasn't been sold

### If you force him to sell stock to pay taxes, you:

- Crash the stock price
- Destroy retirement accounts
- Kill jobs
- Reduce tax revenue

**The real question: What's "fair"?**

### The top 1% pay 42% of taxes but get:

- Same roads
- Same military protection
- Same courts
- Same police

They pay exponentially more for the same services.

That's not "fair share." That's carrying everyone else.

Thomas Sowell said it best:

**"I have never understood why it is 'greed' to want to keep the money you have earned but not greed to want to take the money that somebody else has earned."**

The rich already pay their fair share.

The left just wants more.
