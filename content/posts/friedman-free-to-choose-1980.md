## Milton Friedman's Free to Choose

In 1980 Milton Friedman released a 10-part PBS series that did the impossible:

**He made free-market economics entertaining.**

### Friedman took his camera crew everywhere:

- Hong Kong (freest economy, fastest growth)
- India (socialist disaster)
- Scotland (Adam Smith's birthplace)
- American schools (government monopoly failure)

Each episode demolished a leftist myth:

**Episode 1**: The power of the market creates prosperity, not government plans.

**Episode 5**: Created equal means equal rights, not equal outcomes.

**Episode 9**: Government cure is worse than the disease.

His message was simple:

Freedom works. Coercion fails. Every time.

The book became a bestseller. The series reached millions. Reagan quoted him constantly.

Friedman proved you could win the argument _and_ win the audience.

Socialists have never recovered.
