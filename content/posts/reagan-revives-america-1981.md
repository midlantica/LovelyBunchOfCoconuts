## Reagan Revives America 1981

1980 America: 13% inflation, 21% interest rates, 7% unemployment, "malaise," hostages in Iran.

Reagan slashed top tax from 70% to 28%, deregulated energy, airlines, trucking, and backed Volcker's war on inflation.

1983–1989 results:

- 21 million new jobs
- Inflation down to 3%
- Longest peacetime expansion ever
- USSR bankrupted trying to match American growth

"Reaganomics" was classical liberalism on the world stage: lower taxes, sound money, fewer rules = explosive prosperity.
