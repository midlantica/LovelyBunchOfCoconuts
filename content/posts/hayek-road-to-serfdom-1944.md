## Hayek's Road to Serfdom 1944

In 1944, while the world fought Nazis and Soviets, Friedrich Hayek warned the West was walking the same path.

Central planning—even with good intentions—kills freedom.

Why? Plans need coercion. Someone must allocate resources. Dissent threatens the plan, so dissent is crushed.

Published in 1944, the book predicted nationalization → price controls → shortages → dictatorship.

Britain's 1945–51 Labour government followed the script almost perfectly.

Hayek proved socialism and freedom cannot coexist. A bestseller that later armed Thatcher, Reagan, and millions.
