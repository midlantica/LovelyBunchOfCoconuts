## Thatcher Turns Britain 1979

1979 Britain: 29% inflation, unburied dead, three-day work week, IMF bailout—the "sick man of Europe."

### Margaret Thatcher applied classical liberalism with an iron will:

- Sold council houses to tenants
- Broke inflationary union power
- Privatized steel, coal, telecoms, airlines
- Cut top tax rate from 83% to 40%

By 1990: inflation <3%, unemployment falling, GDP growth doubled, home ownership soared.

Britain went from basket case to Europe's strongest economy.

Proof that liberal policies rescue even deeply socialist nations.
