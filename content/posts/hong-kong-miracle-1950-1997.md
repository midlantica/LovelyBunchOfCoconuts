## Hong Kong Miracle 1950-1997

In 1950 Hong Kong was a barren rock with no natural resources and a million refugees fleeing communist China.

By 1997 it was one of the richest places on Earth.

What happened?

**The freest economy in the world.**

Hong Kong's formula was radical:

- No tariffs
- No capital controls
- Minimal regulation
- Low, simple taxes
- Rule of law
- Property rights

The government's role: Stay out of the way.

The results were staggering:

- GDP per capita grew 180-fold
- Life expectancy jumped from 60 to 80
- A fishing village became a global financial center

Milton Friedman called it "the best example we have of a free-market economy."

Socialists said it couldn't work. "You need planning! You need industrial policy! You need government investment!"

Hong Kong proved them wrong.

Freedom + property rights + rule of law = prosperity.

Every. Single. Time.

Then China took over in 1997 and started strangling it.

The lesson remains: Freedom works. Socialism destroys.
